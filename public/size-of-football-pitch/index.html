Compare any area to the size of a Football Pitch

According to FIFA regulations, a football pitch should be between 90 metres (100 yds) and 120 metres (130 yds) long and 45 metres (50 yds) to 90 metres (100 yds) wide.

A standard size adopted by many well known professional football clubs and some leagues is 105m x 68m. So the area of a standard football pitch can be taken to be 7,140 square metres (76,900 sq ft, 0.714 hectares)

More importantly, the size of a football pitch has long been used as handy comparative unit to measure areas. E.g. Heathrow Aiprort is the size of 1,700 football pitches.
