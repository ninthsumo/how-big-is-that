The new Routemaster came into service in 2012 but it's the original that comes to mind when we think of London's cultural heritage. The original, iconic London Routemaster was manufactured from 1954 - 1968 and was in mainstream service until 2005.

It was 4.38 m (14feet 4.5 inches) tall.

Apart from being a London icon, the double decker bus is often used as handy comparative unit to measure height.

e.g. the Burj Khalifa is as high as 189 double decker busses.
